New environmental nontoxic agents for the preparation of core-shell magnetic nanoparticles

Chekanova, Anastasia E., Sorkina, Tatyana A., Alexander, L. Dubov, Nikiforov, Vladimir N., Davydova, Galina A., Selezneva, Irina I., Goodilin, Evgeny A., Trusov, Lev A., Korolev, Victor V., Aref'ev, Ilya M., Perminova, Irina V., Tretyakov, Yuri D.

Mendeleev Communications, 2009, 19, 72-74, doi: 10.1016/j.mencom.2009.03.006

Abstract

Coal humic acids are successfully tested for the first time as new nontoxic agents for the preparation of core-shell iron oxide magnetic nanoparticles.
